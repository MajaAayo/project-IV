Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly or annual subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period.

How do I access premium books?

Once subscribed, all premium books will be automatically unlocked in your library. You can read them online or download them for offline reading.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, e-sewa, Khalti, and bank transfers through NIC Asia Bank.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all subscription plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.

Can I share my subscription?

No, subscriptions are for individual use only. We have family plans coming soon that will allow sharing with up to 5 family members.

Will I get access to new books?

Yes, your subscription includes access to all new premium books added to our library during your subscription period.
